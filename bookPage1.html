You Like It Darker

$59.99 AUD
★★★★★

You Like It Darker" follows Paul and Emily, a couple who move to the isolated town of Shadeland to escape their past. They settle in an old mansion that soon reveals its sinister secrets. As strange events unfold, they uncover the town's history of mysterious disappearances and dark secrets. Determined to uncover the truth, Paul and Emily face resistance from the townsfolk and supernatural forces within the mansion. Their journey into Shadeland's dark past reveals a web of deceit and evil that they must confront to survive. Scott Carson's thriller explores themes of grief, redemption, and the human propensity for darkness, keeping readers hooked with suspense and unexpected twists.
